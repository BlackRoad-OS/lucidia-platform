FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What frameworks do you support?

We support React, Vue, Angular, Svelte, and vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. Our design tokens work with any framework or no framework at all.

Can I customize the color palette?

Absolutely. While our default palette is carefully crafted, you can override any color token to match your brand's unique identity.

Is there a Figma library?

Yes! We maintain a comprehensive Figma library that stays in sync with our code components. Updates are pushed automatically.

How do I get support?

Free users have access to our community Discord. Paid plans include priority email support and dedicated Slack channels for Enterprise.

What about accessibility?

All our components meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards out of the box. We're committed to making the web accessible to everyone.

Can I use this for client projects?

Yes, all our licenses allow commercial use. You can use Schematiq for client work, internal tools, or public products.
